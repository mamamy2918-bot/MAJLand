About MAJ the Author

A passionate storyteller with a gift for weaving tales that resonate with the human experience.

Myara A. Jefferson, known professionally as MAJ the Author, was born on February 4, 1992, in Tallahassee, Florida. As one of sixteen children in a blended family, Myara learned early about the complexities of relationships, family dynamics, and finding one's voice in a crowded world.

During her teenage years, Myara faced significant personal challenges, including struggles with depression that led her to a breaking point. In her darkest moments, she discovered that writing offered a path to healing and hope. Through poetry and short stories, she found she could escape her immediate reality and enter what she came to call "the Land of MAJ" â€” a creative sanctuary where pain transformed into art, and vulnerability became strength.

This transformative relationship with writing became the foundation of her literary career. The pen name "MAJ" represents more than just initials; it symbolizes her journey from despair to empowerment, from isolation to connection. Her work authentically explores themes of mental health, resilience, love, and the human capacity for transformation.

Today, MAJ the Author channels her experiences into compelling contemporary romance and literary fiction that doesn't shy away from difficult subjects. Her writing speaks to readers who have faced their own struggles, offering both entertainment and emotional validation. Her debut novel, "Anticipation: Love Me, Lust Me, But Don't Lose Me," explores complex relationships and the courage required for authentic intimacy.

Beyond her writing career, Myara is passionate about education and community service. She is the future owner of Touch A Heart Teach A Mind Preschool LLC, reflecting her commitment to nurturing young minds and hearts. Whether through her literary work or her educational endeavors, her mission remains consistent: to help others in any way possible, using her experiences to light the way for those who might be struggling in their own darkness.

Their deep connection and undeniable chemistry create a romance that challenges everything they thought they knew about love, desire, and commitment.

As Melia and Brandon's relationship deepens, they must confront their past traumas, fear of vulnerability, and the question that haunts all lovers: Is it possible to love completely without losing yourself? With the support of her village and the complexities of modern life, their journey explores the courage required for authentic intimacy and the transformative power of true love.

Anticipation: Love Me, Lust Me, But Don't Lose Me is a raw, honest portrayal of contemporary relationships that explores themes of passion, identity, polyamory, and the transformative power of authentic connection. MAJ the Author crafts a story that is both deeply sensual and emotionally intelligent, challenging readers to examine their own beliefs about love, desire, and what it means to truly know another person.

Meet the Characters

The compelling souls at the heart of "Anticipation"

Melia

Melia (Amelia) is the emotional center of our story, a young woman deeply in love with Brandon. When we meet her in Chapter 4, she's dealing with the aftermath of an intimate moment, searching for her phone and discovering missed calls that could have changed everything. Her raw emotions, from tears of regret to hopeful anticipation, show us a character who loves deeply and fears losing what matters most.

Passionate Vulnerable Authentic

Brandon

Brandon is Melia's devoted partner, a working man whose love messages reveal his deep affection and desire. In Chapter 4, his text calling their time together "phenomenal" and expressing his eagerness to return shows a man genuinely in love. His ten missed calls demonstrate his concern and commitment, making him the kind of partner who won't easily stay away from the woman he loves.
